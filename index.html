Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how the Personal Whoop Automation application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects data accessed from your WHOOP account. This is a personal automation tool developed for individual use to integrate WHOOP recovery data with workflow automation systems.

2. Developer Information

Developer: Shahad Eissa
Contact: shahadcodesforai@gmail.com
Purpose: Personal health data automation and optimization

3. Data Collection

The App accesses the following data from your WHOOP account through the official WHOOP API:

  • Recovery Data: Recovery scores, HRV, resting heart rate, respiratory rate, and recovery metrics
  • Cycle Data: Daily physiological cycle information including strain and sleep data
  • User Profile: Basic profile information (name, email) as provided by WHOOP

4. How We Use Your Data

Your WHOOP data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Generating personalized workout recommendations based on recovery scores
  • Creating automated daily briefings and health summaries
  • Triggering workflow automations based on recovery status
  • Personal health tracking and analysis

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is handled as follows:

  • Storage: Data is temporarily processed in workflow automation systems (n8n) and may be stored locally on secure personal servers or cloud infrastructure
  • Retention: Historical data may be retained for personal tracking purposes. You can request deletion at any time.
  • Security: Access tokens and credentials are stored securely using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Access: Only you have access to the data. No third-party services have access to your WHOOP data unless explicitly configured by you.

6. Data Sharing

Your WHOOP data is NOT shared with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No data is sold to third parties
  • No data is shared for marketing purposes
  • No data is shared with analytics services
  • Data remains private to your personal automation workflows

Exception: If you configure integrations with other services (e.g., Notion, email, messaging apps) through your workflow automation, data will be sent to those services as you explicitly configure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all data accessed by the App at any time
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of all stored data by contacting the developer
  • Revocation: You can revoke the App's access to your WHOOP data at any time through the WHOOP mobile app or by contacting the developer
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your processed data in a machine-readable format

8. Third-Party Services

This App relies on the following third-party services:

  • WHOOP API: For accessing your health data. Subject to href="https://www.whoop.com/us/en/full-privacy-policy/" target="_blank" >WHOOP's Privacy Policy
  • n8n (or similar workflow automation): For processing and routing data according to your configured workflows

Any additional services you choose to integrate are subject to their own privacy policies.

9. Data Processing Location

Data processing occurs in the following locations:

  • Personal servers or cloud infrastructure in Kuwait
  • Data does not leave the configured infrastructure unless you explicitly route it to external services
